Our Story

Somnium Reborn is a cutting-edge gaming studio dedicated to creating immersive, innovative experiences that push the boundaries of interactive entertainment. Our team of passionate developers, artists, and designers work collaboratively to bring unforgettable stories and gameplay to life.

Our Team

Allen Asher

Co-Founder & Creative Director

I have a huge passion for videogames and had a dream to create a company that was for gamers and for developers. My imagination is my favorite part to be sent to unique and vast worlds that open adventure and challenges players. My goal is to create the greatest videogame publishing and developing company the world has ever seen. To be different that these corporate money focused companies out there. God is at the center of my vision and with his help I know we will succeed.

Kevin Frazier

Co-Founder & Technical Director

I've been a software engineer lead for years and have always been passionate about game development. I've self-taught myself the skills needed to build the games that help bring the vision that both Allen and I want to contribute to - make games that changes lives.
